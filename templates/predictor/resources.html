Emergency Information

Diabetes Emergency

If you or someone else is experiencing a diabetes emergency:

  • Call emergency services (911 in the US)
  • If the person is unconscious, check for a medical ID bracelet or necklace
  • Do not give food or drink to an unconscious person
  • If the person is conscious and has low blood sugar, give them a fast-acting carbohydrate

Signs of a Diabetes Emergency:

Confusion or difficulty speaking
Sweating and shakiness
Extreme thirst or frequent urination
Loss of consciousness
